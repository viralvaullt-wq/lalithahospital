About Us

A Legacy of Healing & Excellence

Founded in 2025, Lalitha Multispeciality Hospital has grown into one of Hyderabad's most trusted healthcare institutions, combining cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate human care.

Our 50-bed facility houses state-of-the-art operating theatres, intensive care units, and diagnostic centres — staffed by highly trained physicians, nurses, and support staff.

Our Specialities

Comprehensive medical care across a wide spectrum of specialisations

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac care including interventional procedures, electrophysiology and heart surgery.

Neurology

Comprehensive neurological diagnostics and treatment for stroke, epilepsy and movement disorders.

Orthopedics

Joint replacements, sports medicine, spine surgery and advanced trauma care.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care with chemotherapy, radiation and surgical oncology services.

Pediatrics

Specialised care for children from newborns through adolescence in a child-friendly environment.

Gynecology

Women's health covering obstetrics, gynecological surgery and fertility treatments.

Ophthalmology

LASIK, cataract surgery, retinal treatments and comprehensive eye care services.

Pulmonology

Treatment of respiratory conditions including asthma, COPD and sleep disorders.
